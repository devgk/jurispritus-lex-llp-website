Ms. Vinita Sahay

Associate Partner

Career

Vinita has been in the legal profession for more than two decades and holds an extensive experience of litigation related to Civil, Criminal and Commercial Law. She appears before various District Courts, Tribunals, Commissions and the Delhi High Court. A decade of practice in the field of litigation as an Advocate and being empanelled with various government and non-government organizations like BSNL, Indian Oil, ICICI Bank, Northern Railways, Life Insurance Corporation. She joined the Delhi Dispute Resolution Society, (DDRS), Dept. of Law, Justice & Legislative Affairs, Govt. of NCT of Delhi, as an Additional Director, and worked on the benefits of society and received several letters of appreciation from the various government agencies.

As a passionate advocate for women empowerment, she is dedicated to her work with the Corporates on the prevention of sexual harassment at the workplace (POSH). She helps the International Human Rights and Crime Control Forum as a senior consultant. She has also been nominated as the President of Arbitration and Conciliation Council, Women’s Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry, Delhi, India.
