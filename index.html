Welcome to Jurisperitus Lex LLP

Jurisperitus Lex LLP is a new generation full service law firm having its office at New Delhi, with a pan India presence. The members of the firm provide a perfect blend of legal acumen and pragmatic approach derived from immense practical and hands on experience in diverse fields of law. This is coupled with an innovative approach in providing solutions to delicate disputes and transactional legal needs within the often complex Indian regulations, with an interdisciplinary and collaborative approach which involves working with chartered accountants and professional from other disciplines. Ashutosh Kumar (Managing and Founding Partner) specializes in Corporate, Civil and Cyber laws. Manish Kumar Choudhary (Principal and Founding Partner) primarily deals with Civil and Criminal cases.

Providing innovative, pragmatic and business friendly solutions to the clients is the motto of the firm. Finding the best legal solution means looking beyond traditional approaches and thinking creatively. The firm believes in setting new standards in client-centric legal practice with availability, consistent high quality and complete legal solutions.
