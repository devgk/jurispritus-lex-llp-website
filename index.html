Welcome to Jurisperitus Lex LLP

Jurisperitus Lex LLP is a decade-old law firm established in 2013 with the right blend of experienced and dynamic professionals with expertise in their respective areas of practice and young and enterprising lawyers. The Firm and its professionals possess vast and varied experience in civil commercial litigation, alternative dispute resolution, IPR litigation and registration, real estate transaction advisory, indirect taxation, merger and acquisition, insolvency and bankruptcy practice and various legal/regulatory compliances.

The partners & senior professionals of the Firm have over 25 years of extensive and comprehensive experience in their respective fields of expertise with well established credentials in advising domestic and international clients. The Firm has pan-India presence through its network of experienced and seasoned legal professionals in all jurisdictions across the length and breadth of the country.

The Firm specialises in providing innovative and out of the box solution to complex legal issues designed to protect and safeguard the interests of the clients.
